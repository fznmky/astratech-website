Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Astratech Solutions collects professional information necessary to facilitate business-to-business procurement services. This includes corporate contact details, engineering schematics, and supply chain requirements voluntarily submitted through our portal.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for feasibility analysis, supplier matching, and executing procurement contracts. We do not sell, rent, or lease your corporate data to third-party marketing agencies.

3. IP Protection & GDPR Compliance

We enforce strict data silos. Technical drawings are only shared with audited manufacturers after a mutually signed Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is executed. European clients have full rights to data deletion under GDPR Article 17.
